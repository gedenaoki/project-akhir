Line Follower

Line follower robots are one of the most popular robotics applications, especially in the context of learning and competition. This robot is designed to follow a predetermined path, using sensors integrated into its electronic circuit. With its adjustable capabilities, line follower robots are an ideal choice for experiments in the development of automation technology. Below, we will review more about the line follower robot circuit, the components needed, how it works, and the steps in making it.
